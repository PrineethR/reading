# A New Way to Read

Welcome to my new home on the web. 

For the longest time, I've struggled to find a platform that feels right for my writing. Everything out there is either too noisy, too opinionated, or too eager to pull you away to the next trending article. I needed a space that respected the time of the reader.

This site is an experiment in simplicity. No databases, no complex backends, no tracking pixels. Just pure, unadulterated HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript serving up Markdown. 

## The Architecture

If you're reading this, you are seeing the result of a very deliberate design choice. I write my posts in Markdown, exactly like this one:

```markdown
Hello, World!
This is a snippet of how I format my thoughts.
```

The site fetches these files directly and parses them in the browser. It acts exactly like a CMS without the heavy lifting. I can just drag a `.md` file into my `content/` folder, update the `posts.json` index playfully, and it instantly becomes part of the archive.

> "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication." - Leonardo da Vinci

### Features
1. **Readable Typography:** A combination of Merriweather for the body and Inter for the headings. They pair beautifully and read elegantly on both desktop and mobile.
2. **Subtle Interactions:** Notice the custom cursor that softly follows you? Or the gentle fade-in as you scrolled this page into view? GSAP helps us add those micro-interactions that make a site feel premium without being overbearing.
3. **Dark Mode:** A reading experience shouldn't hurt your eyes at night. Try toggling the theme in the top right corner.
